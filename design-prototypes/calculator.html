Understanding Your Pockets

Here's what each pocket means and how to use it effectively

Savings Pocket

This is money you set aside for future needs and goals. Start with building your emergency fund, then work toward other savings goals like a vacation, car, or down payment.

Lifestyle Pocket

This covers your daily living expenses like food, transportation, entertainment, and personal items. Track your spending to make sure you stay within this amount.

Emergency Fund

A safety net that covers 3-12 months of mandatory expenses. This protects you from unexpected events like job loss, medical emergencies, or urgent repairs.
